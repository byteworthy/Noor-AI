## 1. Support Philosophy {#philosophy}

### Core Principles

**1. Empathy First**
- Users are learning Islam/Arabic - be patient and encouraging
- Never dismiss concerns, even if they seem minor
- Assume positive intent

**2. Speed + Quality**
- Quick acknowledgment (within 4 hours)
- Thoughtful resolution (within 24 hours)
- Better to take time than give wrong Islamic info

**3. Education > Transactions**
- Teach users how to use features
- Link to help articles
- Turn support into learning moments

**4. Scholar Oversight for Religious Questions**
- Never provide fatwa/religious rulings yourself
- Escalate to advisory scholars
- Always include disclaimer

### Support Team Structure

**Phase 1 (Launch - phase): Solo Founder**
- You handle all support initially
- Use templates and automation
- Target: 50-100 tickets/week

**Phase 2 (development phase): Hire Part-Time Support**
- 1 part-time support specialist (20 hrs/week)
- You handle religious questions
- Target: 200-500 tickets/week

**Phase 3 (phase+): Full Support Team**
- 1 full-time support lead
- 1-2 part-time agents
- 1 Islamic scholar (consulting basis)
- Target: 500-1000 tickets/week

## 5. Escalation & Bug Triage {#escalation}

### Issue Priority Levels

**P0 - Critical (Fix Immediately)**
- App crashes on launch for all users
- Payment processing completely broken
- Data loss / corruption
- Security vulnerability
- Offensive content displayed

**Response:** Within 1 hour, fix ASAP (even if 3 AM)

---

**P1 - High (Fix Within 24 Hours)**
- Core feature broken (Quran won't load, AI won't respond)
- Payment issues affecting multiple users
- Incorrect Islamic information
- Major UI bug affecting UX

**Response:** Within 4 hours, fix within 24 hours

---

**P2 - Medium (Fix Within 1 Week)**
- Minor feature broken (audio playback glitch)
- UI inconsistencies
- Performance issues
- Translation errors

**Response:** Within 24 hours, fix in next update

---

**P3 - Low (Backlog)**
- Feature requests
- Nice-to-have improvements
- Minor UI polish
- Documentation updates

**Response:** Acknowledged, added to roadmap

## 8. Crisis Management {#crisis}

### Crisis Scenarios & Response Plans

---

**Scenario 1: Incorrect Islamic Information Goes Viral**

**What:** User screenshots AI giving wrong Islamic answer, posts to Twitter, goes viral

**Response Plan:**
1. **Immediate (Within 1 hour):**
   - Acknowledge publicly: "We're aware of this error and investigating"
   - Disable AI feature temporarily if needed
   - Alert Islamic advisory board

2. **Within 4 hours:**
   - Post official statement on all channels
   - Explain what happened
   - Commit to fix timeline

3. **Within 24 hours:**
   - Fix deployed
   - Public apology with explanation
   - Offer free premium to affected users

**Template Response (Twitter):**
```
We're aware of the incorrect Islamic information shared by our AI. This is unacceptable and we've temporarily disabled the feature while we investigate.

Our commitment: Every answer must be accurate and sourced. We're working with our scholarly advisors to fix this ASAP.

Updates: [link to status page]
```

---

**Scenario 2: App Store Rejects Update**

**What:** Apple/Google rejects app update for guideline violation

**Response Plan:**
1. Read rejection reason carefully
2. If religious content issue:
   - Prepare appeal with scholar endorsements
   - Provide sources for all religious content
   - Emphasize educational purpose
3. If technical issue:
   - Fix and resubmit immediately
4. Communicate to users:
   - "Update delayed, expected [date]"

---

**Scenario 3: Security Breach / Data Leak**

**What:** User data compromised

**Response Plan:**
1. **Immediate:**
   - Contain breach (shut down affected systems)
   - Notify legal team
   - Begin forensic investigation

2. **Within 72 hours (GDPR requirement):**
   - Notify affected users by email
   - File breach report with authorities
   - Offer free identity protection (if sensitive data)

3. **Public communication:**
   - Be transparent about what happened
   - Explain what data was affected
   - Detail steps taken to prevent recurrence

---

**Scenario 4: Negative Press / Bad Reviews**

**What:** Tech blog publishes critical article about Noor AI

**Response Plan:**
1. Read article carefully - is criticism valid?
2. If valid:
   - Acknowledge: "They're right, we're working on it"
   - Commit to specific improvements
3. If invalid/unfair:
   - Respond professionally with facts
   - Provide context
   - Offer to discuss with journalist

**Don't:**
- Get defensive
- Attack the critic
- Ignore it
